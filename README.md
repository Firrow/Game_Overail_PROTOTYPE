# over_rail

Here is the prototype of my first indie game: Overail 
In this game, you play as a train on a closed rail network. Your goal is to eliminate the other trains using your weapon and the items you find on the tracks.
Play solo against AI opponents or with your friends.
More game modes will also be added.
You can test the last release on the repo available here : https://github.com/Firrow/Releases-Overail-Prototype
